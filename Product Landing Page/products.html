POCO X3 NFC

The POCO X3 NFC is a mid-range smartphone officially launched on September 7, 2020. It features a 6.67-inch FHD+ display with 120Hz refresh rate, 20 Megapixel punch-hole selfie camera, and a 64MP + 13MP + 2MP + 2MP quad rear camera system. It runs on a Qualcomm Snapdragon 732G chipset with 6GB of RAM, Android 10 OS, and MIUI 12 software. There's also a side-mounted fingerprint scanner, 4G LTE connectivity, stereo speakers, NFC, IR blaster, and either 64GB or 128GB of expandable storage. A 5,160mAh battery powers the device with support for up to 33W fast charging technology via USB Type-C. (PinoyTechnoGuide)
